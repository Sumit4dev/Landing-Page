What is SAP ?

What is SAP software used for?

Traditional business models typically manage data in a decentralized manner, where each department maintains its own separate database. This fragmented approach makes cross-functional data access challenging for employees and leads to duplicate records across departments—driving up IT storage costs and increasing the likelihood of data inconsistencies.

SAP software is used to streamline and integrate business processes across various departments, providing a centralized platform for data management and analysis. It helps organizations improve efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance decision-making by offering real-time insights into key business metrics.
